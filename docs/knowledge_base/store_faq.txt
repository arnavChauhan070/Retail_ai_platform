WALMART RETAIL STORE — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)
=========================================================

SECTION A: GENERAL STORE QUESTIONS
-------------------------------------

Q: What are your store hours?
A: Most stores are open Monday–Saturday 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and Sunday 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
   During holiday weeks (Thanksgiving, Christmas), stores open at 6:00 AM and close at midnight.

Q: How many store locations do you have?
A: We operate 45 store locations. Our top-performing stores by annual revenue are:
   Store 20 ($301M), Store 4 ($299M), Store 14 ($289M), Store 13 ($286M), and Store 2 ($275M).
   Our smallest stores include Store 33 ($37M) and Store 44 ($43M) annually.

Q: Which months are your busiest?
A: December is our busiest month with average weekly sales of $1,281,864.
   November is second at $1,147,266/week due to Thanksgiving and Black Friday.
   January is our slowest month at $923,885/week average.

Q: Do sales go up during holidays?
A: Yes. Holiday weeks generate on average $1,122,888 in weekly sales — 7.8% higher
   than non-holiday weeks ($1,041,256). We track Super Bowl, Labour Day,
   Thanksgiving, and Christmas as major holiday events.

SECTION B: RETURNS & REFUNDS
------------------------------

Q: What is your return policy?
A: Standard return window is 90 days with receipt. Electronics are 15 days.
   Holiday purchases (Nov 1 – Dec 24) can be returned until January 31.

Q: Can I return something without a receipt?
A: Yes. We issue store credit at the current lowest sale price. A valid government-issued
   ID is required for no-receipt returns.

Q: How long does a refund take?
A: Refunds to original payment method take 3–5 business days.
   Cash refunds over $50 are issued as store credit or cheque.

Q: Can I return grocery items?
A: Perishable grocery items are non-returnable unless defective or spoiled at purchase.
   Packaged non-perishable groceries can be returned within 90 days with receipt.

Q: What about clearance items?
A: Clearance items are final sale and cannot be returned or price-adjusted.

SECTION C: PRICING & PROMOTIONS
---------------------------------

Q: Do you price match?
A: Yes. We match identical items from competitors within 7 days of purchase.
   Bring proof of the competitor's current price (ad, website screenshot, etc.).

Q: What is a Rollback price?
A: Rollback is a temporary price reduction valid while stock lasts. These are not
   permanent price changes and will revert once stock is cleared.

Q: When is Black Friday?
A: Black Friday falls the day after Thanksgiving (fourth Thursday of November).
   Our stores open at 6:00 AM for doorbuster deals. Limited quantities apply.
   November is our second-highest revenue month at $1,147,266 average weekly sales.

Q: Do you offer bulk discounts?
A: Yes. A 5% discount applies to orders of 10 or more units of the same non-grocery item.

SECTION D: LOYALTY PROGRAM
----------------------------

Q: How does Walmart Rewards work?
A: Earn 1 point per $1 spent. 100 points = $1 store credit. Minimum redemption is 500 points.
   Earn 3x points during Thanksgiving and Christmas holiday weeks.
   Points expire after 12 months of inactivity.

Q: How do I sign up for Walmart Rewards?
A: Sign up free at the customer service desk or through the Walmart app.

Q: Are there bonus point events?
A: Yes. 3x points during holiday weeks. 2x points on store-brand products every Saturday.

SECTION E: PAYMENTS & LAYAWAY
-------------------------------

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: Cash, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, Walmart Gift Cards,
   Walmart Pay, and EBT/SNAP.

Q: Do you offer layaway?
A: Layaway is available October through December for electronics and toys.
   A 20% deposit is required. Final payment must be made by December 15.

Q: Can I pay by personal cheque?
A: Yes, up to $200 with a valid government-issued ID.

SECTION F: INVENTORY & STOCK
------------------------------

Q: What if an item is out of stock?
A: Ask any staff member to check back-of-store inventory. If unavailable, we issue
   a Rain Check valid for 30 days, or you can place a special order (2–3 weeks lead time).

Q: When do you restock for the holidays?
A: We increase inventory levels by 35% ahead of November and December.
   Our highest-revenue stores (Store 20, Store 4, Store 14) maintain deeper
   inventory year-round to meet consistent high demand.

Q: What are your average weekly sales figures?
A: Across all 45 stores and 6,435 weekly records (2010–2012):
   - Overall average weekly sales: $1,046,965
   - Peak (December): $1,281,864/week
   - Lowest (January): $923,885/week
   - Holiday weeks: $1,122,888/week
   - Non-holiday weeks: $1,041,256/week
   - Total recorded revenue: $6.74 billion

SECTION G: ONLINE & APP
-------------------------

Q: Can I shop online?
A: Yes. Visit walmart.com or use the Walmart app for online orders,
   price checks, store pickup (free), and home delivery.

Q: Does the app show store inventory?
A: Yes. The Walmart app shows real-time aisle locations and stock levels
   for your local store.

Q: How do I contact customer service?
A: In-store desk (front of store), phone: 1-800-WALMART (7 days, 7AM–11PM),
   or 24/7 online chat at walmart.com.
