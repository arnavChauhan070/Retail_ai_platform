WALMART RETAIL STORE — OFFICIAL STORE POLICY
=============================================
Last Updated: January 2026

1. STORE OPERATING HOURS
-------------------------
Standard Hours  : Monday to Saturday 7:00 AM – 11:00 PM
Sunday Hours    : 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM
Holiday Hours   : Extended hours during Thanksgiving week and Christmas week.
                  Stores open at 6:00 AM and close at midnight during Black Friday week.
                  Christmas Eve closes at 6:00 PM. New Year's Day opens at 9:00 AM.

Note: Holiday weeks (Super Bowl, Labour Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas) account for
7% of all annual weeks but generate 7.8% higher average weekly sales than regular weeks.
During peak December, average weekly sales reach $1,281,864 — the highest of any month.

2. RETURN & REFUND POLICY
--------------------------
Standard Return Window : 90 days from date of purchase with original receipt.
Electronics            : 15-day return window. Must be in original packaging.
Grocery / Perishables  : Non-returnable unless product is defective or spoiled on purchase.
Clothing & Apparel     : 90 days. Tags must be attached. Washed items not accepted.
Holiday Purchases      : Items bought between November 1 and December 24 can be
                         returned until January 31 of the following year.
Refund Method          : Original payment method within 3–5 business days.
                         Cash refunds over $50 issued as store credit or cheque.
No Receipt Returns     : Store credit issued at the current lowest sale price.

3. PRICING & PROMOTIONS POLICY
--------------------------------
Price Match     : We match competitor prices on identical items within 7 days of purchase.
Rollback Prices : Temporary price reductions valid while stock lasts.
Clearance Items : Final sale. No returns or price adjustments on clearance.
Rain Check      : Issued if an advertised item is out of stock during the promotion period.
Bulk Discount   : 5% discount on orders of 10+ units of the same item (non-grocery).

Holiday Promotions:
  - Black Friday (November): Doorbuster deals starting 6:00 AM. Limited quantities.
  - Christmas Week (December): Average store sales peak at $1,281,864/week — our busiest period.
  - Super Bowl Week (February): Electronics and snack promotions. Avg sales $1,053,200/week.
  - Labour Day (September): Back-to-school clearance. Avg sales $989,335/week.

4. LOYALTY PROGRAM
-------------------
Program Name    : Walmart Rewards
Earning Rate    : 1 point per $1 spent in-store or online.
Redemption      : 100 points = $1 store credit. Minimum redemption: 500 points.
Bonus Points    : 3x points during holiday weeks (Thanksgiving, Christmas).
                  2x points on store-brand products every Saturday.
Expiry          : Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity.
Signup          : Free. Available in-store at customer service or on the Walmart app.

5. PAYMENT POLICY
------------------
Accepted Methods: Cash, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover,
                  Walmart Gift Cards, Walmart Pay (app), EBT/SNAP.
Cheque Policy   : Personal cheques accepted up to $200 with valid government-issued ID.
Layaway         : Available October through December for electronics and toys.
                  20% deposit required. Final payment by December 15.

6. STOCK & INVENTORY POLICY
-----------------------------
Out of Stock    : Staff will check back-of-store inventory on request.
Special Orders  : Available for large appliances and furniture. 2–3 week lead time.
Rain Check      : Valid for 30 days from issue date.
Inventory Peaks : Stores stock up ahead of holiday weeks. November and December
                  inventory levels are 35% higher than the annual average.
                  Top revenue stores (Store 20: $301M, Store 4: $299M, Store 14: $289M)
                  maintain deeper inventory year-round.

7. CUSTOMER SERVICE
--------------------
In-Store Desk   : Located at the front of every store. Open during all store hours.
Phone Support   : 1-800-WALMART. Available 7 days a week, 7:00 AM – 11:00 PM.
Online Chat     : Available on walmart.com 24/7.
Complaint Escalation: Store manager available on request. Regional manager contact
                      available at customer service desk.
